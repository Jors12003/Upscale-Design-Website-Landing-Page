Our Story

Built on Vision,
Driven by Excellence

Upscale Builder Services Inc. was founded with a single purpose — to bring world-class design and construction to the Philippines. What began as a small team of passionate architects and builders has grown into a full-service firm trusted by businesses, homeowners, and government institutions alike.

Over the past decade, we have completed over 150 projects across commercial, residential, and government sectors — each one a testament to our commitment to quality, precision, and timeless design.
